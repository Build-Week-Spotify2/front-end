Having trouble finding music to suit the mood?

With the Spotify Song Suggester finding the right music to fit your mood is right at your fingertips with a click of a button. Feeling down? Choose from a list of options that tells what your looking for.

Do you want to know what your friends are listening to?

The Spotify Song Suggester app will not only help you choose the music you like listening to. But also connect with your friends to see what they are currently listening to and even there previous songs.

Intrested in lisening to new music?

When you use the Spotify Song Suggester the app will keep track of your favorite and previously listened to songs. The app then makes new song recomendon based on your previous song history.
